Meet Karla

Karla is a student at Spelman College and has been struggling with her bio chemistry class lately. She feels that she has been studying sufficiently for the course, and has even been attending study groups in the evenings. Her roommate recommends that she tries the 10K learning app to track her study times.

Study smarter, not harder!

She begins to use 10K learning to track her study times and focus levels. After just a week of using it, she sees that she's surpassing the recommended weekly study time by 4 hours! She also notices that 80% of her study time is low focused and in the evenings. As a result, she experiments with going to bed earlier and waking up early to study. After just a few weeks her scores are improving, she's studying less and has even dropped the evening study groups!

Meet Sam

Sam is a project manager at a local advertising agency. When he was a teenager he played the keyboard and has been struggling to get back into it. A lack of time is not his problem, even with a full-time job, a wife and 2 daughters he finds he has time to practice, he just can't seem to get motivated. A coworker recommends he tries the 10K learning app.

Practice with friends

After using the 10K learning app for only a few days, he finds himself encouraged by the points and leveling up system. But what he finds most intriguing is the location aware learning feature that allows him to see others practicing in his area. Seeing that Steve is practicing every morning helps him to get up earlier to put in some time on the keys as well.
